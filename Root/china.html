China Today

China is one of the few countries in the world that is reporting low daily infection rates. Chinas's daily reported cases peaked in February 2020 with about 5,000 confirmed cases. It is also the starting point of the coronavirus, where it appeared in the city of Wuhan at the Huanan Seafood Wholesale Market. The first reported cases happened in early December 2019.


China's total number of reported cases is over 86,000 as of November 2020, with over 81,000 recovered and over 4,000 deaths.

Since then, the number of cases have dropped and only reporting a few cases everyday. China's response to the virus was the launching of testing, tracing and community lockdowns, while also investing money into developing vaccines, therapy, and tests.
