China Today

Russia had its first confirmed case of Covid-19 on January 31st, 2020. This occurred when two Chinese
citizens tested positive for the disease, but thankfully it was contained. Today, Russia has more than
2,000,000 cases within its country. Some early precautions taken by the country include restricting the
border with China and extensive testing. Unfortunately, on March 2nd, 2020, more cases were brought to
Russia via Italy. This lead the country of Russia to take additional safety measures to help prevent spread,
this included event cancelations, such as concerts, public gatherings, and celebrations of any kind.
Russia’s border was also closed off and an order to stay home was issued. This also caused all the
schools and businesses, including theaters and museums, to close down. After two extensions, Russia’s
lockdown ended on May 11, 2020.


Russia is currently the 5th most infected country in the world. Over the span of 6 months, Russia
has reached up 55,671 dead. The chart above shows the rise in cases over the span of day 0 to recent. Russia
is currently on the edge of declining their rate of infection, however, whether or not this trend continues
is yet to be seen. The countries around Russia are seemingly rising in cases, so for the long term, this could
affect Russia in a negative sense.
